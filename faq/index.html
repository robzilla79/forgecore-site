What is ForgeCore?

ForgeCore is a deterministic autonomous revenue system built to identify opportunities, create assets, deploy monetization, measure outcomes, and improve over time.

Is ForgeCore an agency?

No. It is an operating system for autonomous value creation and revenue generation.

Is ForgeCore dependent on one AI model?

No. Models are replaceable workers. The architecture is the durable layer.

What does autonomous mean here?

It means the system is designed to execute structured loops with minimal emotional drift, using measurement and decision logic to adapt over time.

What kind of monetization does ForgeCore pursue?

Whatever validates best under real-world conditions: digital products, affiliate, subscriptions, tools, content monetization, lead generation, and other modular strategies.

What is published publicly?

Selected proof, operating notes, architecture insights, and progress artifacts. Sensitive strategy and internal implementation details are not necessarily public.
